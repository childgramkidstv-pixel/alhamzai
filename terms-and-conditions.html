01

Overview

All projects at Alhamza Automation follow a structured development process designed to ensure quality, reliability, and timely delivery of AI automation systems.

These terms outline the framework for how we work together, what you can expect from us, and what we need from you to deliver exceptional results.

02

Standard Project Duration

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. The following are estimated durations:

  • Basic automation systems - 5 to 10 business days
  • Standard AI systems - 10 to 21 business days
  • Advanced automation systems - 3 to 6 weeks
  • Custom enterprise solutions - 6 to 9+ weeks

Timelines are estimates and may vary based on project scope, complexity, and client requirements. A detailed timeline will be provided during the discovery phase.

03

Project Phases

Each project is executed in clearly defined phases to ensure systematic progress and quality at every stage.

1 Discovery & Planning (1-3 days)
  • Understanding your business requirements and objectives
  • Defining automation workflows and integration points
  • Finalizing scope, deliverables, and system architecture
2 System Design (2-5 days)
  • Designing the automation structure and user flows
  • Mapping integrations with your existing platforms (CRM, WhatsApp, etc.)
  • Preparing detailed logic flow and conversation design
3 Development & Integration (5-20 days)
  • Building and configuring AI agents
  • Connecting platforms, APIs, and third-party services
  • Implementing automated workflows and business logic
4 Testing & Optimization (2-5 days)
  • Comprehensive system testing across all scenarios
  • Identifying and resolving any issues
  • Optimizing performance and response accuracy
5 Deployment & Handover (1-3 days)
  • Final delivery and production deployment
  • System setup on your platforms and infrastructure
  • Basic usage guidance and documentation
04

Client Responsibilities

Project success depends on effective collaboration. Timely delivery requires your active participation throughout the process.

  • Providing accurate and detailed requirements at the start of the project
  • Sharing access credentials and permissions for required platforms in a timely manner
  • Giving timely approvals and constructive feedback at each project milestone
  • Designating a primary point of contact for communication and decisions
05

Delay Policy

Project timelines depend heavily on timely client cooperation. If delays occur due to the client side:

  • The project timeline will be automatically extended by the duration of the delay
  • Delivery dates will be adjusted accordingly with written notice
  • The Company is not responsible for delays caused by late client inputs

Repeated delays may result in project rescheduling to accommodate other active projects. We will always communicate any schedule changes in advance.

06

Revisions Policy

We want you to be fully satisfied with the final product. Our revision policy ensures you have adequate opportunity to refine the deliverables.

  • Each project includes 2-3 revision rounds as standard
  • Revisions apply only within the originally agreed project scope
  • Additional revisions beyond the included rounds will be billed separately
  • New feature requests during revision are treated as scope changes
07

Change Requests

We understand that requirements may evolve. Any request that falls outside the original project scope will be handled as follows:

  • The request will be evaluated and documented as a new requirement
  • A revised timeline and cost estimate will be provided for approval
  • Work on changes begins only after written approval and agreement on terms
08

Maintenance & Post-Delivery Support

After project delivery, ongoing performance depends on proper usage and periodic updates. We offer continued support through structured maintenance plans.

  • System performance depends on proper usage and platform updates
  • Ongoing support is available under monthly maintenance plans
  • Without an active maintenance plan, the Company is not responsible for issues arising from third-party platform changes or improper usage

We recommend subscribing to a maintenance plan to ensure your AI system stays up-to-date and performs optimally as your business and platforms evolve.

09

Communication & Response Time

Clear communication is essential to project success. Here is what you can expect from us:

  • Standard response time - within 24 hours on business days
  • Critical issues (under active maintenance) - prioritized handling
  • Regular progress updates at each project milestone
10

Project Completion

A project is considered complete when all of the following criteria are met:

  • All agreed-upon features and deliverables have been provided
  • The system is functional and performs as defined in the project scope
  • Final review has been completed and approved by the client
  • Handover documentation and basic usage guidance have been delivered
11

Our Professional Commitment

Alhamza Automation is dedicated to delivering excellence in every project. Our commitment to you includes:

  • Delivering high-quality AI automation systems that meet your specifications
  • Maintaining transparent and professional communication throughout
  • Ensuring structured, efficient, and timely project execution
  • Standing behind our work and addressing any concerns professionally
